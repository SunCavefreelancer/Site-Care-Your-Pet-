Our Services

Laboratory diagnostics
Animalia Veterinary Clinic conducts a wide range of tests on blood, urine and other biological fluids and tissues.
Radiography
Radiography is the study of the internal structure of objects that are projected onto a medium using x-rays.
Endoscopy
A method for examining the internal cavities of the body using special optical instruments - endoscopes.
Surgery
Surgery - translated from Greek literally means hand and work.
Therapy
Therapy is a word derived from the Greek language, which means treatment, healing, medicine.
Ophthalmology
Ophthalmology is a branch of veterinary medicine that studies eye diseases and develops methods of treatment and prevention.
Hospital
Hospital The Animalia clinic has premises, as well as enclosures with warm booths for inpatient treatment, or hotel services for dogs.
Cardiology
Cardiology is the science that studies the heart and cardiovascular system of animals. This includes the structure and development of the heart and blood vessels, their functions and diseases.
Dentistry
Dentistry - deals with the study of teeth, oral cavity, border areas and neck.
